TEA PACKAGAING

This project consisted of selecting a tea company and creating a new tea product. We stuck to a tea company's design elements and ethos. We came up with a target audience and based our colors, design, tea, and fonts on our audience. I decided to create a new line of Stash-branded Eco-Cups, which are ecologically friendlier versions of K-Cups. I created two flavors to reach the needs of my target audience through its flavor, health benefits, and imagery.

DESIGNER SPOTLIGHT

For this project, we had to research, and write a short report on a selected graphic designer. I decided to base my project on Lucille Tenazas. By sticking to Lucille's sporadic typographic style, use of white backgrounds, and use of yellow, I was able to imitate Lucille's style. I bound my book through the technique of Japanese Stitch Binding using fabric from the Philippines, Lucille's native country.
