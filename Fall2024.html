FALL 2024 WORK

With this project, we researched the process of how an item is made from scratch to how it ends up on a store shelf. We had to visualize and make a poster based on these steps The goal of the project was to only use icons (no words) to describe each step. I chose to go with the process of a tennis ball as I played tennis in high school and still enjoy the sport.

SEQUENTIAL INFORMATION

For this project, we focused on text layout. We also were taught about character and paragraph styles. Those two factors were the main focus of this project. We had to find recipes to create our cookbooks, layout the recipe's text in an appealing way, and find appropriate imagery to go with the recipes. The project also required a theme and color scheme. I stuck with a simple but elegant theme with a grayish-blue pallet. I knew instantly I wanted to make a cheesecake cookbook as it's my favorite dessert.
