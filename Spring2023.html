DESIGN YOUR FACE

For this project, the goal was to translate the typographic aspect of a message into a visual form for an event. We had to explore various ways to express the importance of the message while also learning how to use grid systems.We also designed an announcement poster for the event that required us to pick a historical design style. I based my poster on Bauhaus design.

TYPOGRAPHIC HIERARCHY

This project consisted of choosing a font designer and incorporating their font into an informational brochure. Along with writing a brief paragraph about our designer, we had to create a concept based on the font. The brochure I created is based on the font, Bodoni created by Giambattista Bodoni.

TYPOGRAPHICAL EXPERIMENTATION

Bodoni is highly renowned today as a font used in the fashion and magazine industry, because of its modern-style elegance. I incorporated a magazine design into my brochure to make it appear as such.
